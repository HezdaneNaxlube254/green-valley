About Green Valley Academy

Our History, Mission, Vision & Leadership Team

Our History

Founded in 2005, Green Valley Academy began as a small preparatory school with just 50 students and 5 teachers. Our founders, a group of experienced educators, envisioned a school that would provide quality education rooted in Kenyan values while embracing global best practices.

Over the years, we have grown significantly, expanding our facilities and curriculum to meet the evolving needs of Kenyan learners. In 2017, we became one of the first schools in Nairobi to fully implement the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), positioning us at the forefront of educational innovation in Kenya.

Today, Green Valley Academy serves over 600 students from Pre-Primary to Junior Secondary, with plans to expand to Senior Secondary. We remain committed to our founding principles while continuously improving to provide the best possible education for our learners.

Our Mission

To provide holistic, quality education that nurtures every child's intellectual, physical, emotional, and social development, preparing them to become responsible citizens and lifelong learners in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in Kenya recognized for academic excellence, character development, and innovation in teaching, producing well-rounded graduates who contribute positively to society.
